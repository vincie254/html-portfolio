Shows to watch this weekend!

The weekly review by Ken

Power Book 2

This is a continuation of the heated drama pack dubbed "Power". It takes us through to what happens after the death of the arguable plot master in the original series: Ghost. Tariq, the son to Ghost, now runs the show. Its my top recommendation this week. Watch it.

Sistas

This is a drama series by 4 hot women that live by the sister code. It varies from the arguments over men, to unique life choices by the women and what is significantly outlayed; is the support they have for each other. Danny will certainly crack your ribs, especially on the things she does when high. Watch it as well.

All American

This series is catchy to al the baseball lovers. Its a twist of love, love for the game, love for the ballers, the survival through trafficking of drugs, and more. I recommend. Please watch it as well.
